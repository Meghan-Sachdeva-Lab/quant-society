Our Mission & Plan

Welcome to the Quant Society! Our goal is to connect mathematics, computer programming, and finance. Here is what we plan to do this semester:

  • Step 1: Learn basic Python coding to fetch real-time stock market closing data.
  • Step 2: Study stock price history and calculate moving averages (finding trends!).
  • Step 3: Build simple algorithmic strategies (like buying when the price crosses the 10-day average).
  • Step 4: Backtest our strategy using fake paper trading accounts to see if we make a profit.
